Vishal Mishra

Vishal Mishra has firmly established himself as one of the most soulful and distinctive voices in the contemporary Hindi film playback singing scene. While he began his journey as a music composer, it was his raw and powerful vocal talent that catapulted him to widespread recognition. He is celebrated for his ability to infuse immense emotion and a rock-edged grit into his performances, making every song he sings feel intensely personal and heartfelt. His breakthrough came with the massively popular "**Tera Ban Jaunga**" from *Kabir Singh*, and he has since delivered a string of unforgettable hits like "**Kaise Hua**" from *Kabir Singh*, "**Naina**" from *Crew*, and the introspective "**Samjhawan**" (Unplugged). Not just a singer, but also a skilled composer and lyricist, Vishal Mishra's multifaceted talent and passionate delivery make him a standout artist in the new generation of Indian music.

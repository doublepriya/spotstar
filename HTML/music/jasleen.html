Jasleen Royal

Jasleen Royal is a breath of fresh air in the Indian music industry, celebrated as a versatile singer, composer, and multi-instrumentalist who brings a distinctive indie-inspired sensibility to her Bollywood work. She first captured widespread attention with her soulful performance in the popular YouTube cover "**Din Shagna Da**," which perfectly showcased her sweet, melodious voice and acoustic charm. This organic talent seamlessly transitioned to cinema, where she has composed and sung some of the most memorable and hummable tunes in recent years, including the joyful wedding anthem "**Nachde Ne Saare**" from *Baar Baar Dekho*, the breezy "**Love You Zindagi**" from *Dear Zindagi*, and the poignant "**Kho Gaye Hum Kahan**" from *Baar Baar Dekho*. Blending folk-infused melodies with contemporary arrangements, Jasleen Royal has carved a unique niche for herself as an artist whose music feels both intimately personal and universally appealing.

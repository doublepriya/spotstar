Kausar Munir

Kausar Munir is one of the most versatile and respected voices in the Indian film industry, carving a distinct niche for herself as a prolific lyricist and screenwriter known for her strong, nuanced, and often female-centric narratives. While she initially gained recognition for her impactful lyrics in films like *Ishqzaade* ("**Pareshaan**") and *Ek Main Aur Ekk Tu* ("**Auntyji**"), her talent for storytelling soon took center stage. She demonstrated a remarkable ability to craft compelling scripts that are both entertaining and meaningful, as seen in the hit family comedy *Badrinath Ki Dulhania* and the empowering musical drama *Secret Superstar*. Her work often infuses commercial Bollywood tropes with greater depth and social consciousness, whether through her witty, character-driven dialogues or her songs that advance the plot. Blending the sensibilities of a writer and a poet, Kausar Munir has become a powerful creative force behind some of the most successful and substantive films in contemporary Hindi cinema.

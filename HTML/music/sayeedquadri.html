Sayeed Quadri

Sayeed Quadri is one of the most profound and poetic lyricists in contemporary Hindi cinema, celebrated for his ability to weave intricate emotions and profound philosophy into his verses. With a career that took a definitive turn through his collaborations with composer M.M. Kreem and later with Pritam, Quadri masterfully blends Urdu poetry with modern sensibilities, creating songs that are both deeply literary and widely accessible. He is the voice behind some of the most iconic and introspective songs of the past two decades, including the existential masterpiece "**Tum Ho**" from *Rockstar*, the heart-wrenching "**Aaj Din Chadheya**" from *Love Aaj Kal*, the playful yet meaningful "**Pungi**" from *Agent Vinod*, and the soul-stirring "**Kun Faya Kun**" from *Rockstar*. His lyrics often explore themes of love, longing, spiritualism, and the human condition, earning him a revered place as a wordsmith who doesn't just write songs, but crafts timeless poetry for the silver screen.

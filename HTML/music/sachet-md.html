Sachet Tandon

Sachet Tandon, alongside his musical and life partner Parampara Thakur, has rapidly ascended as one of the most formidable and sought-after music composer-singer duos in modern Bollywood. Initially gaining notice as a contestant on a reality music show, his career catapulted to new heights with the raw, angst-filled power of "**Bekhayali**" from the blockbuster *Kabir Singh*, a performance that showcased his ability to channel intense emotion through his gritty, resonant vocals. This breakthrough paved the way for a series of hits that he both composed and sang, including the soulful "**Mere Sohneya**" (*Kabir Singh*), the electrifying "**Barbaadiyan**" (*Shiddat*), and the devotional mega-hit "**Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar**" (*Adipurush*). Known for his remarkable versatility—effortlessly shifting from rock-edged fervor to gentle melody—Sachet embodies the new generation of multifaceted artists who are shaping the soundscape of Indian cinema today.

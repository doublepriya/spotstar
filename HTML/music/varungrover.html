Varun Grover

Varun Grover stands as a singular and intellectually vibrant voice in contemporary Indian culture, seamlessly excelling as a screenwriter, lyricist, satirist, and novelist. He first gained widespread acclaim for his sharp, poetic, and often groundbreaking work as a lyricist in films, penning the rustic and philosophical "**Moh Moh Ke Dhaage**" from *Dum Laga Ke Haisha*, the bittersweet "**Ghar**" from *Jab Harry Met Sejal*, and the deeply moving "**Kaamyaab**" title track. Beyond music, his genius shines in his screenwriting; he was the creative force behind the critically adored and socially resonant anthology *Masaan* and the wildly popular series *Sacred Games*, which redefined Indian web entertainment.

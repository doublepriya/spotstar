Sachet Tandon

Sachet Tandon, along with his musical partner Parampara Thakur, has rapidly emerged as one of the most successful and celebrated music composer-singer duos in modern Bollywood. He first gained significant attention as a contestant on a reality music show, but his true breakthrough came with the monumental success of the song "**Bekhayali**" from the blockbuster *Kabir Singh*, which showcased his ability to convey intense, raw emotion through his powerful and gritty vocals. This was followed by a string of hits that he both composed and sang, including the romantic "**Mere Sohneya**" from *Kabir Singh* and the devotional chart-topper "**Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar**" from *Adipurush*. Known for his versatile voice that can seamlessly transition from rock-infused anguish to gentle melody, Sachet Tandon represents the new generation of multifaceted musicians who are defining the sound of contemporary Indian cinema.

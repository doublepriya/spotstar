Alia Bhatt

Alia Bhatt is not just a star but a formidable force in contemporary Indian cinema, having carved a unique path defined by both critical acclaim and commercial success. With a career launched as a teenage star in Karan Johar's "Student of the Year", she swiftly dismantled any notions of being just a glamorous fixture, showcasing a startling and profound acting range in films like "Highway", "Udta Punjab", and "Raazi". Her ability to completely immerse herself in diverse roles—from a vulnerable kidnap victim to a gritty Punjabi migrant and a resilient Indian spy—has established her as one of the most versatile and talented performers of her generation. Beyond acting, she has emerged as a successful producer and a influential voice for modern women, building a brand that is both relatable and aspirational, making her a true powerhouse of the new Bollywood.

Kevin Feige

As President of Marvel Studios, he transformed a library of B-list comic book characters into a global pop culture phenomenon, meticulously crafting an interconnected narrative across dozens of films and television series. His deep reverence for the source material, combined with an unparalleled understanding of franchise storytelling and audience engagement, has resulted in an unprecedented string of blockbusters, from "Iron Man" to "Avengers: Endgame". Under his leadership, the MCU has not only redefined the modern blockbuster but also consistently championed diversity and innovation with films like "Black Panther" and "Shang-Chi".
